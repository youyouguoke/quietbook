A gentle hands-on guide through a child's day. Children move activity cards through morning, school and bedtime routines, building independence and predictability.

## What's Included

- Morning Routine sequencing
- Bedtime Routine sequencing
- Get Dressed matching
- Brush Teeth checklist

## Skills Developed

- **Life Skills:** Practice daily routines.
- **Sequencing:** Order events in the right sequence.
- **Fine Motor:** Move cards and place Velcro pieces.
